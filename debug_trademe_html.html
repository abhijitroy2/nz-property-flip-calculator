Description

Auction: 34 Shortland Street, City on Friday 7 November 2025 at 10:00AM (unless sold prior)

Set well back from the road in a quiet and private position, this traditional 1960s solid Kiwi bungalow is a gem. Loved and cherished by the current owners for over 13 years, it’s now ready for its next chapter.

Step inside to discover three generous bedrooms, a spacious separate living area, and polished timber floors that bring warmth and character throughout. A brand-new kitchen with adjoining dining area makes everyday living a delight, complemented by a nearly new separate bathroom and toilet.

Outdoors, a spacious undercover deck provides the perfect spot to relax after a busy day or enjoy time with family and friends, whatever the weather.

Positioned for convenience, this home is within walking distance to Avondale College, Avondale Town Centre, and the vibrant Sunday Flea Market. Just a short drive to LynnMall and motorway connections, plus excellent public transport links with buses and trains nearby—this is central-west living at its best.

Whether you’re a first-home buyer, retiree, or investor, this very affordable home presents a wonderful opportunity in one of Auckland’s popular city-fringe suburbs.

The owners are committed elsewhere and focused on selling on auction day—so don’t miss out on securing this special property.
